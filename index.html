💡 How it Works

10% commission credited instantly to agents. The remaining gap commission — based on the agency's final monthly target — is settled in the first week of the following month.

🔥 Example

If monthly income is 25M, final commission = 25M × 14%. Agency receives 10% daily and the remaining 4% (25M × 4%) is paid on the 1st of next month.

Commission Tiers (Hierarchical)
  • Commission rate upgrades immediately when revenue crosses each threshold during the month
  • Example: Revenue below 4,999,999 → 10%. Once it exceeds 5M → rate upgrades to 12%

2
Super Admin Commission Structure
⌄
ℹ️ Overview

A Super Admin can have multiple Admins under them, and can also create and manage their own direct agencies.

  • Earns the same as Admin (5%) from revenue generated by their direct invited agencies
  • Additionally earns 2% commission from the total revenue generated by all Admins under them
  • Super Admin will lose the status of Super Admin if they don't meet the targets defined by the company
💰 Earnings Summary

5% from direct agencies + 2% from all Admins under them

3
Country Admin Commission Structure
⌄
ℹ️ Overview

A Country Admin can manage multiple Super Admins and can also bring their own direct agencies.

  • Earns 1% commission from the total revenue generated by agencies under their entire team (Admins + Super Admins)
  • Also earns 5% commission from revenue generated by their direct invited agencies
💰 Earnings Summary

5% from direct agencies + 1% from entire team revenue

How to Become Country Admin
📌 Requirements

To become a Country Admin, a Super Admin or Admin must either:

  • Reach a minimum of 1,000M revenue, OR
  • Deposit a $5,000 USD security to the company account (fully refundable)
